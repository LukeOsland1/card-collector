Welcome to Card Collector!

A Discord bot for managing collectible cards.

To get started, invite the bot to your Discord server and use the slash commands.

Features:

  • Create and manage collectible cards
  • Assign cards to users with optional expiry
  • View your card collection
  • Role-based permissions for admins and moderators
  • Automatic expiry handling

Commands:

  • /card my - View your cards
  • /card submit - Submit a card for review
  • /card create - Create a card (moderators)
  • /card assign - Assign a card to a user (moderators)
